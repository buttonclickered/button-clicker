Nouns, Verbs, and Adjectives

Nouns

Common Nouns (Des Noms Communs) are things, like a dog, a book, or a house. They do not start with a uppercase.

Proper Nouns (Des Noms Propres) are specific names of people, places, or things, like Marie, Paris, or the Eiffel Tower. They start with a uppercase.

Verbs

Verbs (Des Verbes) are action words, like to eat (manger), to run (courir), or to be (être).

Adjectives

Adjectives (Des Adjectifs) describe nouns, like big (grand), small (petit), or beautiful (beau/belle).

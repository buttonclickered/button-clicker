Prepositions

What are Prepositions?

Prepositions (Des Prépositions) are words that show the relationship between a noun or pronoun and other words in a sentence. They are usually location, direction, or time.

Common Prepositions

Some common prepositions in French include:

  • à (at, to)
  • de (of, from)
  • dans (in, inside)
  • sur (on, upon)
  • sous (under)

Notes







About

This is a simple, useful notes taking website that allows you to save and load your notes easily.
Since it's using localStorage, your notes are kept completely private and stored on your browser, so nobody can see your notes. Not me, not Chrome, nobody

Instructions:

First, name your note in the "Note Title" field.
Write your note in the text area provided.
To save your note, click the "Save Note" button.
To load a previously saved note, enter the title of the note in the "Title of Note to Load" field and click the "Load Note" button.
